What is Reform.

Built for bodies that move like dancers.

Reform. is a boutique pilates studio in the heart of Central — built by a dancer, staffed by dancers, designed around the way dancers train their bodies. Long. Controlled. Precise.

Most pilates studios treat the reformer as a machine. We treat it as an instrument. The difference shows in how you move after class.

Signature class

Hong Kong's first
Barreformer
studio.

Barreformer combines the full-body strength of reformer pilates with the precision and grace of barre technique. The result is a class that targets the muscles dancers use — deeply, specifically, relentlessly.

Led by Libby Liu, full-time dancer at the Hong Kong Dance Company. When she teaches it, you feel the difference in your posture for days.

Barre + Reformer Dancer-led Max 6 All levels HK First
01 — BARRE
Ballet-informed technique
Footwork, turnout, port de bras. The reformer carriage becomes your barre. Posture and precision from the first move.
02 — REFORMER
Full-body resistance work
Springs, straps, and your own bodyweight. The reformer makes every movement intentional — length through resistance.
03 — RESULT
The dancer body
Long, lean, and deeply strong. Not a gym body. Not a yoga body. A body that moves like it was designed to.
