Recommendation
Mindbody — if ClassPass is a priority
ClassPass drives intro client acquisition and the native integration is seamless. HK clients already know the app. But READ the contract carefully — 12–24 month lock-in, prices increase annually. Negotiate hard before signing.
Hapana — if pilates-first features matter more
Built-in reformer spot reservation is a real differentiator. Raised USD 7.25M Jan 2026, actively building. Less HK brand recognition but the product fits Reform. better than any generic fitness platform.
ABC Glofox — best budget option with branded app
From ~USD 100/mo with branded app included — cheapest way to have a professional client-facing product at launch. Stripe integration. Good for keeping costs low pre-opening while still looking polished. Confirm ClassPass integration before committing.
WellnessLiving — Mindbody alternative without the contract risk
Month-to-month available, ClassPass integration, similar feature set to Mindbody at lower cost. Worth getting a quote alongside Mindbody to compare. Less known in HK but strong globally.

§2 — What is Reform.
Founder statement
English
Reform. is a boutique reformer pilates studio in the heart of Central. Founded by Kylie — dancer, gym obsessive, and the person who decided Hong Kong needed a studio built differently.
Most of our instructors are professional or semi-professional dancers. That's not a coincidence — it's the whole point. The way dancers train their bodies is exactly what pilates was designed for. Long, controlled, precise. You feel it in your posture, your lines, the way you carry yourself.
We cap every class at 6. That's not a limitation — it's the point. You get the machine, the space, and the attention you came for.
reset. reform. repeat.
繁體中文
Reform. 係中環嘅精品普拉提工作室。由 Kylie 創辦 — 舞蹈員、健身狂，同埋一個決定香港需要一間唔同嘅工作室嘅人。
我哋大部份導師都係職業或半職業舞蹈員。唔係巧合——係整個概念嘅核心。舞蹈員訓練身體嘅方式，就係普拉提本來嘅設計。修長、控制、精準。你會感受到係你嘅體態、你嘅線條、你行路嘅方式。
每班只收6人。唔係限制，係承諾。你嚟嘅係一部機、一個空間、同埋真正嘅關注。
重置。改變。重複。
